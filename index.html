about obxium

obxium is an artist.

 

Welcome to obxium files

Hello.

obxium files is a small archive of files important to the artist and shared here with you.
The concept for this project was taken directly from the "The Dan Files".

All the files here also live on the Bitcoin blockchain through the power of
inscriptions, part of the Ordinals system for decorating the individual base unit
of Bitcoin, called Satoshis.

The files are presented in the style of Mac OS 9 for joy & nostalgia.


  Tip: If you get stuck and cannot scroll, try the maximize button in the top right area
of any window. You can also just reload the site to start with a fresh state.

Art on 5 blockchains since 2018 @ ↹ atelier obxium 🇫🇷

System.css is MIT licensed
