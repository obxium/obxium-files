obxium files

Artist curated data inscribed on the Bitcoin blockhain

Hello.

obxium files is a small archive of files important to the artist and shared here with you.
The concept for this project was taken directly from the "The Dan Files".

All the files here also live on the Bitcoin blockchain through the power of inscriptions, part of the Ordinals system for decorating the individual base unit of Bitcoin, called Satoshis.
